Program

All talks will take place in the Horace Lamb Lecture Theatre.

Plenary talks (blue) are 50 minutes with 10 minutes for questions.

Contributed talks (orange - in person, yellow - online) are 20 minutes with 5 minutes for questions.

All talks will be recorded and made available for peoples convenience.
