Program

All in person talks will take place in the Horace Lamb Lecture Theatre.

The conference is being run in hybrid mode with zoom link: https://adelaide.zoom.us/j/84193352145?pwd=a1A2V2IzTGNuQUlodUQvL21kVW00Zz09&from=addon. Which will be consistent across all days.

All talks will be recorded and made available at the MOSSI Youtube channel https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCnOLj_4UbAKZV21JT5Lk1YA

Plenary talks are 50 minutes with 10 minutes for questions
In person contributed talks are 20 minutes with 5 minutes for questions
Online contributed talks are 20 minutes with 5 minutes for questions

Presentation Set-up

For in person presentations we encourage you to bring your own device, and use zoom from it to present your talk. The available wi-fi networks are eduroam (if you are familiar) or the University of Adelaide's guest wifi ('UofA Guest' which will have password 'appleground'). You can also bring a usb with your presentation or send it to Jordan via email to jordan.pitt@adelaide.edu.au.

For online presentations you can use zoom as normal. If you have a pre-recorded talk then please contact Jordan via email to jordan.pitt@adelaide.edu.au either with a link to your video or a method to provide the video to ensure the best quality.
